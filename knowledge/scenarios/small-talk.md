# Small Talk Scenario Knowledge

Friendly small talk often moves from a light topic to a personal detail and then a follow-up question.

Safe topics include weather, weekend plans, hobbies, recent meals, travel, work routines, and local events.

Natural follow-up phrases include "That sounds fun", "How did that go?", and "What got you interested in that?"

Avoid overly private questions at the beginning of a casual conversation.
