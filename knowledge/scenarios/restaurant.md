# Restaurant Scenario Knowledge

Polite ordering phrases include "Could I have...", "May I get...", and "Would it be possible to...".

When asking about allergies, mention the ingredient clearly and ask whether the dish can be prepared without it.

Common restaurant follow-up questions include choosing still or sparkling water, selecting a side dish, and deciding whether to split the bill.

If something is wrong with the order, a natural phrase is "Excuse me, I think this may not be what I ordered."
