# Travel Help Scenario Knowledge

Useful travel phrases include "How do I get to...", "Where can I buy a ticket?", and "Is this the right platform?"

When checking into a hotel, travelers usually provide a name, reservation confirmation, and passport or ID.

If transportation is delayed, ask about alternative routes, refund rules, and the next available departure.

For directions, confirm whether the person wants walking directions, public transport, or a taxi.
